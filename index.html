The problem I solve

Finance creators lose viewers
in the first 3 seconds.

Most finance content is educational gold buried inside mediocre editing. Here's exactly what's costing your reels views — and what I fix.

01
Weak hooks that don't stop the scroll

Finance viewers are skeptical and fast. If the first frame doesn't show a number, a bold claim, or a clear problem — they're gone. I open every reel with a pattern-interrupt hook engineered to make them stop and watch.

Hook engineering
02
Long pauses killing retention curves

Raw finance talking-head footage has filler words, gaps, and slow explanations. I cut tight — removing every "um", every pause, and every moment the viewer's attention could drift, without making it feel choppy.

Retention editing
03
No captions = 60% of views lost

Over 60% of reels are watched on mute. Word-by-word animated captions keep muted viewers locked in. I style them to match your brand — not the default auto-caption look.

Animated captions
04
Generic B-roll that doesn't match the script

Stock footage of random money piles doesn't build credibility. I match B-roll precisely to what's being said — charts, market tickers, phone screens, and relevant visuals that make the point land harder.

Finance B-roll
